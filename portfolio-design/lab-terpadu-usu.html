Laboratorium Terpadu USU Brand Design

Brand Design / Social Media / Printing

About

The Laboratorium Terpadu USU at Universitas Sumatera Utara is a technical implementation unit that provides a variety of laboratory testing services. Since 2021, I have been involved as a freelance graphic designer, primarily managing the lab's Instagram account. I create content ranging from event documentation to celebratory posts. Beyond social media, I also design banners, booklets, flyers, and other promotional materials.

Challenge

Handling various types of requests from different users.

Solution

Needed to comprehend user requirements and translate them into visuals that meet their expectations.

Rebranding

As a designer, I took over the work from the previous designer for 2 years. Towards the end of 2023, I was instructed by the management to redesign the Laboratorium Terpadu USU's social media to build a stronger, more recognizable brand and align it with USU's established design system.
