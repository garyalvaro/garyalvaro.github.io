About

Established in 1959, The Department of Civil Engineering Universitas Sumatera Utara is one of the departments within the Faculty of Engineering and is recognized as the first and oldest in the faculty. Until 2021, the department lacked a social media platform to interact with lecturers, students, alumni, and external stakeholders. Following an initiative from the department, an Instagram account was established, and I was selected as a freelance graphic designer to manage it to this day. My duties include creating diverse posts for the department's Instagram, along with captions requiring copywriting skills, and also respond to inquiries from online users through Instagram DMs.
